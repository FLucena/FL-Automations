About me

Sobre mí

Hello! My name is Francisco. I specialize in web development, workflow optimization, and process automation. Over the last 10 years, my passion for new technologies and my quantitative background have driven me to create smart data processing solutions. Imagine waking up in the morning, pressing a button, and having all your recurring tasks ready for you by the time you get your first cup of coffee. I'm here to make it a reality! I have extensive experience in web development using Next.js and React. These technologies enable me to create fast, efficient, and interactive applications that provide an excellent user experience. My commitment is to ensure your satisfaction at every stage of the project. I will work tirelessly until the final result exceeds your expectations. Whether you are starting your first project or looking to take an existing one to the next level, I am excited to turn all your automation dreams into reality. Cheers!
